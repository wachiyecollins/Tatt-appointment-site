Before Your Appointment

To ensure a smooth and comfortable tattoo experience, please follow these guidelines before your session:

  • Eat a full meal beforehand to maintain your energy, especially if itâ€™s your first time.
  • Be confident in your design choice. If you have doubts or need changes, please reach out ahead of time.
  • Minors must present a notarized consent form. This form must be signed and stamped before the appointment.
  • For longer sessions, consider bringing:
    • A pillow if you'll be laying down for a while.
    • Snacks and water to stay hydrated and comfortable.
    • A friend for support (optional, and only for shorter appointments).
  • Avoid direct sunlight on the tattoo for at least two weeks.

Tattoo Aftercare

  • Remove the plastic wrap after 1 hour.
  • Wash the tattoo gently with antibacterial soap (e.g., Dial soap).
  • Wash 2â€“3 times daily during the 3-week healing period.
  • Apply a thin layer of Aquaphor after each wash. Avoid overapplying.
  • Keep your tattoo out of direct sunlight while healing.
  • Do not scratch, pick, or scrub your tattoo.
  • Avoid swimming in pools, lakes, hot tubs, or any water bodies.
  • Short showers are fine; avoid soaking the tattoo in baths.

If you have questions or concerns, feel free to contact us directly.
